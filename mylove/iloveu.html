Happy Birthday My Love








Dear Mine:

I could kiss you a thousand times and still not be satisfied. My love for you is endless, so tender, so hot and complete. You and only you have given me so much hope and have made me realize how much I want you.

Therefore, day after day, I wonder why, I wonder how, I wonder where you are. Seeing you will cause me an indescribable thrill, even at the sight of your pictures will make me tremble.

And wonderful times we shared together shall alway remain in my heart. You are my little angel and sunshine. Just having you close fills me with love and hope. It is only when I nearly lose you that I become fully conscious of how much I value you.

Accoringly, I would say, “I love you” for millions and billions of times, and times and times again. No matter how long the road may be in the futher, please cherish every moment we shared together. No matter how many years will pass away, please treasure our love till the last day. Love is the triumph of imagination over intelligence.

Every comes and goes, but love stays. When you need someone, remember that I’d be there.
We would get old and get used to each other. We will think alike. We can read each others minds. We know what the other wants without asking. Sometimes we irritate each other a little bit. Maybe sometimes take each other for granted. But once in awhile, like today, I meditate on it and realise how lucky I am to share my life with the greatest woman I ever met.

Happy birthday my love.
Leo
